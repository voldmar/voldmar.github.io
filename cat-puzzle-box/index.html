Project · Cat puzzle box

A box of holes, for a cat.

Six laser-cut sheets that build into a 380×280×100 mm plywood box. Seven round top holes, two L-shaped cutouts, and twelve side holes, sized for paws. A sandpaper lining so the surface can take a grip.
